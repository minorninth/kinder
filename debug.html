Kindergarten Escape

High score: 46.1 seconds
Instructions

This is an audio game. Headphones are strongly recommended, but good stereo speakers could work too.

In this game, the kindergarten kids are dismissed from school first. The kindergartener is about to leave when she realizes she forgot her teddy bear. She runs back to get it, hearing the sounds of different objects and classrooms along the way.

After she finds her bear, the lights turn out and she has to find her way back out. As she passes each classroom, sometimes something will be wrong - an anomaly.

  • Rule 1: If she hears an anomaly, she must turn around before reaching the classroom door.
  • Rule 2: If there is no anomaly, she must keep walking.

If she makes the wrong decision, she ends up back at the kindergarten class again.

Controls

Keyboard: Hold Up Arrow to walk, tap Down Arrow once to turn around.

Touch screen: Once the game begins, the top half of the screen will be the Walk button and the bottom half will be Turn Around. If you're using a screen reader, double-tap and hold the Walk button in order to keep walking. You don't have to hold the Turn Around button.

Tips
  • The game doesn't progress if you stop moving. Take your time approaching each door until you get better at the game.
  • The order of the classrooms is always the same.
  • An anomaly might introduce an extra sound, or it might replace the sound of a classroom with a different one.
  • Rarely, the anomaly might be that the classroom door is on the wrong side.
